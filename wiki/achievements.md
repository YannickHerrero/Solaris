# Achievements

Solaris features **260 achievements** across 8 categories. Each achievement unlocked provides a **+0.5% multiplicative production bonus**.

## Achievement Bonus

The achievement bonus applies to all production:

```
Achievement Multiplier = 1.005^n
```

Where `n` is the number of achievements unlocked.

| Achievements | Bonus |
|--------------|-------|
| 10 | +5.1% (1.051x) |
| 25 | +13.3% (1.133x) |
| 50 | +28.4% (1.284x) |
| 100 | +64.9% (1.649x) |
| 150 | +111.8% (2.118x) |
| 200 | +171.5% (2.715x) |
| 260 | +267% (3.67x) |

## Achievement Hunting Tips

### Easy Early Achievements

1. **One Minute** - Just play for 60 seconds
2. **Novice Solar Panel** - Buy your first Solar Panel
3. **Clicker** - Click Space 100 times
4. **First Thousand** - Earn 1,000 total energy
5. **Small Fleet** - Own 10 total producers

### Achievement Categories by Difficulty

**Easy** (First hour of play):
- First 3-4 time achievements
- First 2-3 click achievements
- Novice/Initiate producer achievements for early producers
- First few lifetime energy achievements

**Medium** (First few runs):
- Most producer achievements up to Expert tier
- Energy per second up to 1M E/s
- First few ascension achievements
- Upgrade collection up to 100

**Hard** (Multiple ascensions required):
- Master/Grandmaster producer tiers
- High-tier producers (Cosmic String onwards)
- Late-game E/s achievements
- 300+ upgrade collection

**Very Hard** (End-game):
- Transcendent tiers for all producers
- Infinite Power (1Q E/s)
- Click Infinite (50M clicks)
- Eternal Ascendant (500 ascensions)

### Prestige and Achievements

**Achievements persist through ascensions!** This means:
- Your achievement bonus carries over
- Each ascension becomes easier with more achievements
- Focus on unlocking achievements before ascending
